Our Story

Celebrating individuality through curated fashion that makes you feel like the best version of yourself.

Welcome to Dear Me

Founded in 2015, Dear Me began as a small boutique in New York City with a simple mission: to help people discover clothing that makes them feel confident and authentic. What started as a passion project has grown into a beloved destination for those who value quality, craftsmanship, and personal style.

Our name, "Dear Me," reflects our philosophy - that getting dressed should be an act of self-love. Each piece in our collection is carefully selected to help you express your unique personality while feeling comfortable and empowered.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do at Dear Me

Quality First

We meticulously vet every brand and item in our collection to ensure exceptional craftsmanship and durability. Our buyers travel the world to discover the finest materials and most talented designers.

Sustainable Practices

We're committed to reducing fashion's environmental impact. 65% of our collection comes from sustainable sources, and we've eliminated single-use plastics from our packaging and operations.

Inclusive Style

Fashion should be for everyone. Our collections range from sizes XXS to 3X, and our stylists are trained to help every body type find flattering, confidence-boosting outfits.

Our Journey

Key milestones in the Dear Me story

2015 - Humble Beginnings

Dear Me opens its first 800 sq ft boutique in Manhattan's Lower East Side. Founder Emily Chen personally selects every item in the initial collection.

2017 - Online Expansion

We launch our e-commerce platform, allowing customers across the U.S. to experience Dear Me's curated collections. Our first viral social media campaign reaches 2M+ people.

2019 - Industry Recognition

Vogue names Dear Me one of "10 Boutiques Changing the Fashion Game." We open our second location in Brooklyn and launch our first exclusive designer collaboration.

2021 - Sustainable Shift

We commit to sustainability, transitioning 60% of our inventory to eco-friendly materials and launching our clothing recycling program.

2023 - Looking Ahead

With our third store opening in Los Angeles and plans for international shipping, we're excited to bring the Dear Me experience to more people worldwide.
